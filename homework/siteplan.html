Site Plan

Initial Planning Document

Purpose Statement

To provide quick and easy access to the weather through multiple devices, so that when you are out living the adventure or competing in your sport events you can be prepared for whatever weather mother nature may throw at you.

Audience Description

  • Demographics: People 8 and older look at this site, income can range from allowance of $25 to anything really. People who look at this site should have at least an elementary education, or a basic knowledge of weather. Geographically people should live somewhere where the weather is always changing and unpredictable.
  • Meeting the audience needs: This site should meet the needs of the audience, by allowing them super quick information about the weather so they can perform there outdoor activity and be prepared for whatever mother nature throws at them. Whether it be rain, snow, or wind.
  • Organization: The site should be very easy to navigate through, The main information should be very visible and you shouldnt have to look for it. Its a quick look and go.
  • Mobile experience: This site should provide an even easier experience because it can be accessed on the go, and it will be very simple and too the point.

Persona

A 19 year old who plays many sports and loves to be outdoors and go on adventures, but he lives in Idaho where the weather is so unpredictable, one day it can be super warm and the next day you are having flash floods, or even in a few hours the weather is completely different. He always likes to know what kind of clothing and equipment he will need for his next adventure, but gathering all the equipment he needs for a certain activity can take some time so he just needs to quickly know what the weather will be like for the next few hours and even days so he can pack to be prepared.
